About Bernice

A nurse first. An aesthetics practitioner second.

I'm Bernice, the founder of Jade Medical Aesthetics and a registered nurse prescriber based at Canalside Complex in Worcester. I came to aesthetics through nursing — which means your safety and wellbeing will always come before anything else.

I set up Jade because I wanted to create a space where people feel genuinely looked after. There's no high-pressure sales here, no rushed appointments, and no cookie-cutter treatments. Just honest, expert care in a calm, relaxed environment.

Whether it's your first time considering aesthetic treatments or you're coming back for a top-up, I'll take the time to understand what you're hoping for — and give you my honest professional opinion on what will work best for you.

How I work

What you can expect

Safety above everything

As a registered nurse prescriber, your health comes first. I take a full medical history before every treatment and will always tell you honestly if something isn't right for you.

Results that look natural

The best aesthetic work is subtle — enhancing your features without changing who you are. My goal is for you to look like the best version of yourself, not a different person.

No pressure. Ever.

Every consultation is free and completely obligation-free. I'll never push treatments you don't need. You're in control of every decision, and there's no rush.

Your treatment, your plan

No two people are the same, and neither are any two treatment plans. I take time to understand your goals and create something that's tailored entirely to you.
