Thinking about it but not quite sure?

Will it look obvious or unnatural?
This is the most common concern, and it's a completely fair one. The treatments I offer are designed to be subtle — enhancing your features rather than changing them. Many of my clients' friends don't even notice they've had anything done; they just say they look well-rested or refreshed.
Is it safe? Who will be doing it?
I'm Bernice, a registered nurse prescriber — which means I'm medically trained, NMC-registered, and fully insured. I take a full health history before every treatment. Your safety isn't just a priority, it's the whole foundation of how I work.
What if I come to a consultation and decide it's not for me?
That's absolutely fine. The consultation is free and there's zero pressure to proceed. Plenty of people come for a chat, ask their questions, and decide they'd like more time to think. That's exactly what the consultation is for.
Will it hurt?
Most clients find treatments surprisingly comfortable. I work carefully and take my time — several clients have described their treatment as "virtually pain free". I'll always talk you through what to expect before we start.

About Bernice

A nurse first

I'm Bernice — a registered nurse prescriber and the founder of Jade Medical Aesthetics. I came to aesthetics through nursing, which means patient safety and wellbeing are the foundation of everything I do.

I set up Jade because I wanted to create a place where people feel genuinely looked after — especially those who are trying something for the first time. No rush, no pressure, no sales tactics. Just a proper conversation about what you're hoping for, followed by expert, careful treatment.

I'm based at Canalside Complex in Worcester and take new clients by consultation. Come and meet me — there's no obligation to book anything.
