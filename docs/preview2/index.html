About Bernice

Nurse-led care you can trust, results that feel like you

I'm Bernice, a registered nurse prescriber and founder of Jade Medical Aesthetics. I set up the clinic because I believe everyone deserves to feel confident in their own skin — and that the journey to get there should be just as comfortable as the result.

Based at Canalside Complex in Worcester, I offer a full range of aesthetic treatments in a calm, unhurried environment. Every appointment begins with a thorough consultation — no pressure, no rush, and no obligation to proceed.

My background in nursing means patient safety always comes first. I take time to understand what you're looking for and offer honest, expert guidance on what will genuinely work for you.

How I work

My approach

Safety first, always

As a registered nurse prescriber, your health and safety are my absolute priority. I take a full medical history before every treatment and will always tell you honestly if something isn't right for you.

Natural results

I believe the best aesthetic work is the kind nobody notices — except you. My goal is always to enhance what you have, not to change who you are.

No pressure, ever

Every consultation is free and completely obligation-free. I'll never push treatments you don't need or upsell during your appointment. You decide, in your own time.

Personalised care

No two faces are the same, and neither are any two treatment plans. I take time to listen to what you want and tailor everything specifically to you.
